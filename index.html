Old to New

Paint isn’t just something that makes your home look good, it also acts as vital protection against the elements. Colorado’s harsh winters and over 300 days of sunshine a year take their toll on the surface of any home. Ultimately, the quality of the paint and its professional application is what will determine how often you need to repaint. 5 to 10 years of protection can be expected from quality materials, while lower quality “big box” and discount supply store paints will drastically reduce that longevity.
